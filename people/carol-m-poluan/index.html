Carol has an extensive experience in the fields of Intellectual Property ("IP"), in particular, with regard to trademark, copyright and industrial design, combined with investigation services and IP enforcement. In addition, she also has years of experience in the fields of corporate and commercial laws. As a litigator she not only covers IP related matters but also that in civil and commercial law in general. Her clients consist of multinational companies, big local corporations, local law firms (for cases where she acts as a consultant) and individuals. Because of her passion in the art and culinary world, she also works closely with a number of art galleries, up-and-comings artists and small businesses in the art and food industries in protecting their IP rights and advising them on the potentials to grow their skills and/or businesses.
