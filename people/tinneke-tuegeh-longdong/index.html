Prof. DR. MR. Tineke Tuegeh-Longdong is one of only a very few lecturers and law practitioners in Indonesia that has in-depth knowledge and extensive experience in corporate law, arbitration & litigation, and in particular, in Indonesia International Private Law. She started her career as the academic assistant of the late Prof. MR. Dr. Sudargo Gautama (Gouw Giok Siong), the father of Indonesia International Private Law, at the then Rechts Hogeschool and continued to be his assistant when the institution became the Faculty of Law of the University of Indonesia. Simultaneously, she was also a lawyer at Prof. Dr. MR. Sudargo Gautama (Gouw Giok Siong) & Associates Law Office. She then became the managing partner of the firm until it was dissolved not long after Prof. Dr. MR. Gautama passed away in 2008. Since then she has set up her own practice. Her clients consists of multinational companies, in particular Japanese companies, and big local law firms. She is also often asked by other reputable law firms to act as a consultant or expert witness in the cases that they handle.
